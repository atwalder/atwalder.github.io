Worcester Arts Workshop: A Sustainable Future

  • My interactive qualifying project (IQP) with the Worcester Arts Workshop (WAW) in Worcester, England spanned from March to May 2017.
  • This IQP involved four students working with a community organization (the WAW) to find ways to improve their operations.
  • We conducted interviews to gather information on organizations similar to the WAW for purposes of comparison; did archival research to better understand WAW’s history, especially its community engagement; and characterized WAW’s use of technology and whether it was operating in an efficient manner.
  • Our written final report to WAW focused on guidelines for WAW to make its operations more cost-effective while improving its standing in the community.
  • We also presented a PowerPoint presentation at the University of Worcester and the Meliora International Symposium of Student Sustainability Research.
